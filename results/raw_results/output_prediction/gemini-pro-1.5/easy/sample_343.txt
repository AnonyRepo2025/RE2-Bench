The `extend` method adds all elements of an iterable to the end of a list.

Initially, `array` is `[[1, 2, 3], [1, 2], 1, [1, 2, 3], 3, [2, 1]]`.
`elem` is `[[1, 2, 3], 3, [2, 1]]`.

After `array.extend(elem)`, the elements of `elem` are appended to `array`.

The resulting `array` will be `[[1, 2, 3], [1, 2], 1, [1, 2, 3], 3, [2, 1], [1, 2, 3], 3, [2, 1]]`.
